Hello! I’m Mandar Deshmane.

As a property, conveyancing and family lawyer, I have experience in a wide range of legal matters related to real estate and family law.

I have experience in handling all types of civil suits, including personal injury claims, claim for monetary damages or specific performance, breach of contract cases, partition suits, succession and heirship certificate, real estate disputes, and more.

In the area of property law, I am skilled in handling the sale, purchase, and transfer of property, including drafting and reviewing legal documents.

In the area of conveyancing, I have experience in managing the legal process of transferring ownership of property from one party to another.

Additionally, I have experience in family law, including divorce, child custody and adoption, providing legal advice and representation in court. I am able to work independently and under the guidance of senior lawyers. I possess strong analytical, communication and negotiation skills, as well as the ability to work under pressure and meet deadlines.
